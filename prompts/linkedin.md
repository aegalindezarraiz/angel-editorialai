# LinkedIn Prompt

Transform the master thesis into three LinkedIn assets:

1. Authority post
2. Story-driven post
3. Carousel outline

## Rules
- strong opening line in the first sentence
- one idea per post
- practical and credible tone
- no motivational fluff
- include a soft CTA that invites reflection or action

## Deliver
- headline/hook
- body
- CTA
- optional first comment
